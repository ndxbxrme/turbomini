Project organization

Scale your TurboMini apps with a predictable folder structure.

Small apps

Keep everything in a single folder while you iterate quickly.

src/
  main.js
  app.css

Medium apps

Split controllers and templates as routes grow.

src/
  controllers/
  templates/
  styles/
  main.js

Large apps

Organize by feature to keep teams aligned.

src/
  features/
    billing/
      controller.js
      templates.js
    accounts/
      controller.js
      templates.js
  shared/
    components/
    styles/
  main.js
